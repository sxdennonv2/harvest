Communication to a High Standard · Course contents

The course map

Writing craft for the whole office, taught to a high standard — and then climbed. Rewriting, reframing and revising for tone, clarity, conciseness and correctness: learned by hand first, so you can judge what the machine writes, then handed up the capability ladder from manual craft to GenAI. Sibling to the application-functionality courses; the boundary rule: if a lesson would survive the app being swapped, it lives here.

Two axes hold the plan together: rows are communication themes, and columns are the capability ladder — the same task, climbing from hand-craft to AI. Lessons bind to contexts (email first; documents, slides and chat later) rather than to one app.

Lessons, by theme

Each lesson is a single self-contained page with a built-in self-check and a shared before/after practice pack.
