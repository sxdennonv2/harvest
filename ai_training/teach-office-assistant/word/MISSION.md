## Why
I'm an office assistant at ALDI — the person who keeps the department running:
correspondence, meeting minutes, training material, templates, records and process.
Much of what I produce goes out under the department's name, so professional polish
matters every time. I want to master Word properly (not just get by), work the
modern way, and use that fluency as a launchpad into GenAI productivity.

## Success looks like
- Produce polished letters, minutes and procedures with proper styles and templates.
- Make formatting consistent and repairable — no hand-spaced headings, no broken lists.
- Maintain the department's templates so everyone starts from the right document.
- Handle tracked changes and comments cleanly when documents go for review.
- Do the same task with AND without AI — then pivot it into GenAI tools for speed.

- Full-time role: lessons must be short and immediately useful (single tangible win each).
