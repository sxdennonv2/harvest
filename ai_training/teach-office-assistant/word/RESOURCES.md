# Word to a High Standard — Resources

Curated, high-trust sources. Knowledge is drawn from here, not from guesses.

> Note: links are canonical, long-stable URLs; verify/trim on next online session.

## Knowledge

- [Microsoft Support — Word help & learning](https://support.microsoft.com/word)
  Authoritative primary source. Use for: styles, TOC, sections, tables, track changes,
  templates, what changed between versions.
- [Microsoft — Customize or create new styles](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/customize-or-create-new-styles-d38d6e47-f6fc-48eb-a607-1eb120dec563)
  The styles foundation. Use for: the skeleton theme.
- [Microsoft — Insert a table of contents](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/insert-a-table-of-contents-882e8564-0edb-435e-84b5-1d8552ccf0c0)
  TOC as a field over headings. Use for: structure & navigation.
- [Microsoft — Track changes in Word](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/track-changes-in-word-197ba630-0f5f-4a8e-9a77-3712475e806a)
  Use for: review & collaborate; markup views, accept/reject.
- [Microsoft — Copilot in Word](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot-word)
  What Copilot actually does in Word (draft, rewrite, summarise; not native buttons) —
  keeps rung 3 honest.
