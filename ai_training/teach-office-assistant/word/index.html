The course map

The document as machinery, taught to a high standard — and then climbed. Styles, sections, tables, track changes, templates: learned by hand first, then handed up the capability ladder from manual craft to GenAI. The prose itself lives in the sibling communication course; if a lesson would survive the app being swapped, it isn't here.
