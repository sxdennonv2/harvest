## Why
I'm an office assistant at ALDI — the person who keeps the department running:
correspondence, meeting minutes, training material, templates, records and process.
Much of what I produce goes out under the department's name, so professional polish
matters every time. I want to master PowerPoint properly (not just get by), work the
modern way, and use that fluency as a launchpad into GenAI productivity.

## Success looks like
- Refine and maintain the department's decks and training material to a consistent standard.
- Fix layout, alignment and branding fast — masters and layouts, not nudging boxes.
- Build clean slides from someone's rough notes without losing their meaning.
- Keep reusable templates the whole team can work from.
- Do the same task with AND without AI — then pivot it into GenAI tools for speed.
