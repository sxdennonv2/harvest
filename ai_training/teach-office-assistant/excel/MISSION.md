## Why
I'm an office assistant at ALDI — the person who keeps the department running:
correspondence, meeting minutes, training material, templates, records and process.
Much of what I produce goes out under the department's name, so professional polish
matters every time. I want to master Excel properly (not just get by), work the
modern way, and use that fluency as a launchpad into GenAI productivity.

## Success looks like
- Build correct, tidy spreadsheets for the team — lists, trackers, simple summaries — without fear.
- Use Tables, sorting, filtering and the everyday formulas confidently and correctly.
- Keep shared workbooks clean: consistent formats, no broken formulas, no mystery tabs.
- Recognise when a workbook I've inherited is fragile, and make it safe.
- Do the same task with AND without AI — then pivot it into GenAI tools for speed.
