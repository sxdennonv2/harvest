## Why
I'm a specialist analyst at ALDI — sustainability metrics, category and compliance
reporting, POST/NBC data. I pull figures out of systems and reports (SAP extracts,
published dashboards, shared workbooks), interpret them, and turn them into the
summaries, statistics and charts my team and leadership act on. I want to master
Word properly (not the patchy, self-taught version I inherited), work the modern
way, and use that fluency as a launchpad into GenAI productivity.

## Success looks like
- Produce reports and technical notes with real structure: styles, headings, captions, cross-references.
- Make documents navigable for reviewers — TOC, numbering that doesn't break.
- Present data in Word (tables, embedded results) cleanly and reproducibly.
- Review others' documents fast with tracked changes and comments used properly.
- Do the same document task with AND without AI — then pivot it into GenAI tools for speed.
