Excel to a High Standard · Course contents

The course map

A specialist analyst's Excel, taught to a high standard — and then climbed. Every skill is learned by hand first, so you have the judgement to check what the machine does, then handed up the capability ladder from manual craft to GenAI.

Two axes hold the whole plan together: rows are task themes a specialist analyst actually faces, and columns are the capability ladder — the same task, climbing from hand-craft to AI. Start at lesson 0001 and work down, or jump to whatever theme you need.
