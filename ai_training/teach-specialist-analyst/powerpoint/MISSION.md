## Why
I'm a specialist analyst at ALDI — sustainability metrics, category and compliance
reporting, POST/NBC data. I pull figures out of systems and reports (SAP extracts,
published dashboards, shared workbooks), interpret them, and turn them into the
summaries, statistics and charts my team and leadership act on. I want to master
PowerPoint properly (not the patchy, self-taught version I inherited), work the modern
way, and use that fluency as a launchpad into GenAI productivity.

## Success looks like
- Turn analysis into decks that carry the finding, not decorate it: one message per slide.
- Build charts and tables that survive being read cold by leadership.
- Work structure-first (outline, sections, layouts) instead of pushing boxes around.
- Reuse a consistent, branded look without rebuilding it every time.
- Do the same deck task with AND without AI — then pivot it into GenAI tools for speed.
