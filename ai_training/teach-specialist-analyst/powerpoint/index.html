The course map

The deck as an artifact, taught to a high standard — and then climbed. Masters, alignment, the Excel seam, templates, the last mile: learned by hand first, then handed up the capability ladder from manual craft to GenAI. What a slide says lives in the sibling communication course; if a lesson would survive the app being swapped, it isn't here.
