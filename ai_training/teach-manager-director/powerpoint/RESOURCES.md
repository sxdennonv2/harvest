- [Microsoft Support — PowerPoint help & learning](https://support.microsoft.com/powerpoint)
  Authoritative primary source. Use for: masters/layouts, Designer, charts, templates,
  presenter view, export, what changed between versions.
- [Microsoft — What is a slide master?](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/what-is-a-slide-master-b9abb2a0-7aef-4257-a14e-4329c904da54)
  The canonical master/layout/placeholder explanation. Use for: the foundations theme.
- [Microsoft — Insert a linked Excel chart in PowerPoint](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/insert-a-linked-excel-chart-in-powerpoint-0d3a2853-4d68-4b62-9d21-e69ee6e394b9)
  The paste-link seam. Use for: charts & visuals; keeping slide data alive.
- [Microsoft — Copilot in PowerPoint](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot-powerpoint)
  What Copilot actually does in PowerPoint (create from prompt/file, Designer,
  summarise) and what it doesn't — keeps rung 3 honest.
