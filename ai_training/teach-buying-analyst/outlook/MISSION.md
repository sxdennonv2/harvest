# Mission: Outlook to a High Standard

## Why
The inbox is where the working day actually happens — and where the survey's diffuse
"productivity" demand lives. This course teaches Outlook the tool: triage, retrieval,
threading mechanics, scheduling and automation. The *writing* that goes in the emails is
deliberately not here — that's the communication course; the boundary rule is that if a
lesson would survive the app being swapped, it isn't Outlook functionality.

## Who it's for
The buying analyst persona first (suppliers, price lists, promos, a director), and the
wider office behind them.

## Success looks like
- A mailbox with one home per mail, maintained by rules rather than willpower.
- Anything findable in under thirty seconds with search operators, not scrolling.
- Threads that carry the right people on To/CC and never grow a reply-all accident.
- Meetings that get accepted because the invite carried the agenda.
- The repetitive filed, flagged and answered in one click.
- The same task done with AND without AI — then handed up the ladder with a check at
  every rung.

## Constraints
- Lessons must be short and immediately useful (single tangible win each).
- Enterprise GenAI available: Aldi:GPT, Microsoft 365 Copilot, GitHub Copilot.
- Windows + desktop (new) Outlook; learners practise mostly in their own mailbox.

## Out of scope (for now)
- Writing craft (tone, tightening, structure) — see `../../communication/`.
- Teams/calendar administration beyond a buyer's own meetings.
