Outlook to a High Standard · Course contents

The course map

Outlook the tool, taught to a high standard — and then climbed. Triage, retrieval, threading mechanics, scheduling and automation: learned by hand first, so you can judge what the machine does in your mailbox, then handed up the capability ladder from manual craft to GenAI. The writing inside the emails lives in the sibling communication course; the boundary rule: if a lesson would survive the app being swapped, it isn't here.

Two axes hold the plan together: rows are task themes ordered as the working day runs (triage → find → write → schedule → automate), and columns are the capability ladder — the same task, climbing from hand-craft to AI.

Lessons, by theme

Each lesson is a single self-contained page with a built-in self-check and a shared mailbox practice pack.
