One channel.json per channel: persona (voice, worldview, hard guardrails) × topic tree × format set. Platform is a binding (X first); modality (text · audio · visual) is a binding on the same terms — av-pilot is the working audio-visual instance. The compound loops

One loop shape, two speeds, five lines

Every line runs produce → signal → instrument → fold, at its own window. The inner loops fold into their own line's frame and skills; the meta loop (bottom band) carries friction from every line into the pattern containers — contract idioms, packet shapes, skill shapes. That band is the laboratory→cash-register pipe: what transfers between lines is pattern knowledge, never content. No tags, no compounding: a unit shipped without its coordinate tags is a unit the pipeline cannot learn from.
